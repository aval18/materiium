Materiium.

A marketplace for excess fabrics, connecting businesses and sewers to reduce textile waste worldwide.

Learn More

A Sustainable Resale Marketplace

With the growing fashion industry, the environmental harm from textile production will continue to increase alongside it, as well as the increasing amounts of textile waste. We wanted to develop a community based solution with both small businesses and sewers in mind.

Repurposing Excess Fabrics

Businesses can sell their excess fabrics from production in bulk, along with individual sewers that can list their fabrics. On Materiium, we can work together to find a home for excess fabrics.

Connecting Businesses and Sewers

Through our business-to-business and consumer-to-consumer interactions, we can build a community of trustworthy buyers and sellers.

Intuitive Interface

Materiium will be accessible through both a website and a user-friendly app, making it easy for anyone to join.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why would a business sell their fabrics?

    We talked with businesses of various scale, and found that small businesses who don't have a market advantage from their unique fabrics are the best target market for our platform. By selling their excess fabric, they can make money back on what may have originally been tossed out, and also qualify (if they repurpose enough textiles) for the Materii brand certification, utilizable for their own marketing purposes to relay their dedication to sustainability.

  • What would the fees be like?

    We talked with the seller and small business community to determine a suitable marketplace fee, and the consesus was that 10% was the optimal rate, so we placed ours at 8% total - consisting of 5% marketplace transaction fees, 3% payment processing fees, and a $0.20 listing fee, all of which would only be paid after the seller has made their first sale.
